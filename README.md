# CodeAlpha_PhishingAwarenessTraining.

Outsmarting Digital Deception & Securing Human Elements in Cybersecurity

---

## 📖 Overview
Phishing is the entry point for **90% of global cyber incidents**.  
This training highlights how attackers exploit the **human element** and how to defend against it.

---

## 🚩 Key Topics
- **[Phishing Attack Cycle](ca://s?q=Phishing_attack_cycle)** – Research → Weaponize → Attack → Exploit  
- **[Email Red Flags](ca://s?q=Spotting_email_red_flags)** – Fake domains, urgency, generic greetings, disguised links  
- **[Fake Websites](ca://s?q=Detecting_fake_websites)** – Typosquatting, complex subdomains, certificate checks  
- **[Social Engineering](ca://s?q=Psychology_of_social_engineering)** – Authority, fear, greed tactics  
- **[Defense Practices](ca://s?q=Proactive_defense_practices)** – MFA, hover & check, verify requests, report instantly  
- **[Types of Phishing](ca://s?q=Types_of_phishing_attacks)** – Spear, vishing, smishing, whaling, clone, pharming  

---

## 📊 Case Studies
- **Twitter Bitcoin Scam (2020)** – $120K stolen via phone spear phishing  
- **Bangladesh Bank Heist (2016)** – $81M stolen via infected email attachment
